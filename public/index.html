About Us

Innovative Technology

We develop cutting-edge software that enables fully autonomous drone inspections of high-tension power lines, combining computer vision and advanced navigation algorithms.

Industry Expertise

Our team collaborates closely with power companies and drone specialists to create solutions that address real-world challenges in electricity infrastructure maintenance.

Sustainable Solutions

We’re committed to improving power grid reliability while reducing environmental impact by replacing helicopter inspections with efficient, automated drone systems.

Autonomous Drone Inspection for Power Lines

Our solution automates the inspection and maintenance of high-tension electrical transmission lines, improving safety, reducing costs, and increasing inspection frequency and accuracy.

Real-Time Anomaly Detection

Our system uses advanced computer vision to detect damaged cables, compromised structures, and other hazards in real-time during inspections.

Fully Autonomous Navigation

Drones follow pre-programmed routes without requiring human pilots, making the process safer and more efficient than traditional methods.

Cost Reduction

By eliminating the need for helicopter flights and specialized drone pilots, our solution significantly reduces operational expenses for power companies.

Environmental Impact

Replacing helicopter inspections with automated drones reduces carbon emissions while maintaining or improving the quality of power line monitoring.

Our Solutions

Autonomous Flight Control

Our proprietary navigation system enables drones to autonomously follow power lines without manual piloting, even in challenging weather conditions and complex terrain.

Real-Time Anomaly Detection

Advanced computer vision algorithms instantly identify issues such as damaged insulators, broken conductors, vegetation encroachment, and structural problems during flight.

Comprehensive Reporting

Automated reports provide maintenance teams with detailed information about detected anomalies, including GPS coordinates, severity classifications, and recommended actions.

Integration Services

Our system seamlessly integrates with existing asset management platforms, ensuring that inspection data flows directly into your maintenance workflow without additional steps.

Fleet Management

Coordinate multiple drones simultaneously with our intelligent scheduling system, optimizing inspection routes and maximizing coverage while minimizing operational costs.

Training & Support

Comprehensive training for your team and ongoing technical support ensure smooth implementation and operation of our autonomous drone inspection system.
